A Little bit About Us!

Hello friends, fans and lovers of Live Audio Production. As I’m sure many are aware our fearless leader, friend and colleague, Craig Campbell, was taken by cancer January 2nd, 2021. He has moved on to the “Big Gig” in the sky. Craig spent the last fifteen or so years building Live Audio Production into a go-to A/V source in the Wood River Valley, and further reaches. Offering sound systems, engineering, lighting and an extensive variety of backline. Under Craig’s guidance, Live Audio Production has supported hundreds of different events, (of all kinds) and audiovisual installations. Weddings, fund raisers, memorials, concerts, even Horse parades! In keeping Craig’s legacy alive, per his wishes, with the help of Craig’s sons and many familiar faces we will continue to run Live Audio Production as it has for years. If you have an event and would like to discuss options, or to just say “Hi”, please shoot us a text message or e-mail and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
